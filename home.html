Welcome to KRIJA

...the AI Hackathon that aims to revolutionize Albania! We believe that Artificial Intelligence has the potential to transform various sectors of Albania, from healthcare and education to finance and agriculture.

Accessibility

We aim to help create solutions that are inclusive and have a positive impact on all members of Albanian society.

Critical Thinking

Solutions that are not only innovative but also practical and effective in solving real-world problems.

Creativity

We encourage participants to explore new ideas and approaches to solve complex problems.

Security

We expect all participants to adhere to ethical and legal guidelines when handling sensitive data.

Our Goal

Our goal is to empower the Albanian tech community to apply AI in novel ways and develop solutions that can make a positive impact on society. Participants are encouraged to think outside the box and explore new ideas, while also leveraging their technical skills and expertise to create robust and scalable solutions.

Schedule

We have a packed schedule for Krija, with a range of exciting events and activities designed to challenge and inspire our participants. Whether you're a seasoned pro or new to the world of AI, we've got something for everyone.
